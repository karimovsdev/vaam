FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products, services, and processes.

We offer a comprehensive range of solar panels to suit various applications and budgets. Our product lineup includes high-efficiency monocrystalline panels, cost-effective polycrystalline panels, and advanced bifacial panels that capture sunlight from both sides. Our panels range from 250W to 700W, catering to residential rooftop installations, commercial projects, and large-scale utility solar farms. All our panels come from Tier-1 manufacturers and are certified to international standards including IEC, TUV, and CE.
While our core business focuses on manufacturing and wholesale distribution of solar panels, we work closely with a certified network of installation partners across multiple regions. We can connect you with trusted, experienced installers in your area who are trained to work with our products. Additionally, our technical team provides full project support including system design, engineering consultation, and on-site supervision to ensure optimal installation quality and performance.
Our solar panels come with a robust dual warranty structure. We provide a 25-year linear performance warranty, guaranteeing that your panels will maintain at least 80% of their rated power output after 25 years of operation. Additionally, we offer a 12-year product warranty covering any manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship. This comprehensive warranty package reflects our confidence in the quality and durability of our products, giving you peace of mind for decades of reliable solar energy generation.

For bulk orders and ongoing partnerships, we also offer dedicated account managers who can provide personalized pricing and flexible payment terms based on your specific requirements.
