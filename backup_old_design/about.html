Who We Are

VAAM Import and Export Trading Co., LTD is a forward-thinking solar panel manufacturer and wholesale supplier dedicated to accelerating the global transition to renewable energy. Established in 2026, we bring fresh innovation and competitive excellence to the solar industry.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities combine cutting-edge technology with stringent quality control to produce high-efficiency solar panels that meet and exceed international standards. We serve a diverse clientele ranging from residential homeowners to large-scale commercial and industrial projects.

At VAAM, we believe that sustainable energy should be accessible, affordable, and reliable. This belief drives everything we do, from product development to customer service.
